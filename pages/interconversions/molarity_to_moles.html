Molarity to Moles Calculator

This calculator converts molarity and volume to moles: moles = molarity × volume (in L)

How it works:

  1. Enter the molarity of the solution
  2. Enter the volume of solution in liters
  3. The calculator computes the moles of solute
moles of solute = Molarity (M) × volume of solution (L)

Calculate Moles from Molarity and Volume

Examples

  • Example 1: Calculate moles in 1 L of 1 M NaCl solution
    moles = 1 × 1 = 1 mole
  • Example 2: Calculate moles in 2 L of 0.25 M HCl solution
    moles = 0.25 × 2 = 0.5 moles
  • Example 3: Calculate moles in 0.5 L of 4 M NaOH solution
    moles = 4 × 0.5 = 2 moles

Important Notes

  • Molarity must be in mol/L (M)
  • Volume must be in liters
  • This gives moles of solute only
  • Molarity changes with temperature due to volume changes

Moles to Mass Calculator

This calculator converts moles to mass using the formula: mass = moles × molar mass

How it works:

  1. Enter the number of moles
  2. Enter the molar mass of the substance in g/mol
  3. The calculator will compute the mass in grams
mass (g) = moles × molar mass (g/mol)

Calculate Mass from Moles

Examples

  • Example 1: Calculate mass of 1 mole of water (H₂O, molar mass = 18 g/mol)
    mass = 1 × 18 = 18 grams
  • Example 2: Calculate mass of 0.5 moles of CO₂ (molar mass = 44 g/mol)
    mass = 0.5 × 44 = 22 grams
  • Example 3: Calculate mass of 2 moles of NaCl (molar mass = 58.5 g/mol)
    mass = 2 × 58.5 = 117 grams

Tips

  • Use the correct molar mass from the periodic table
  • For ionic compounds, use the formula mass
  • The result is always in grams
  • Convert to other mass units if needed (multiply by 1000 for kg, divide by 1000 for mg)

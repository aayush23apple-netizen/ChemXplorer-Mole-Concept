Moles to Molarity Calculator

This calculator converts moles and volume to molarity: molarity = moles ÷ volume (in L)

How it works:

  1. Enter the number of moles of solute
  2. Enter the volume of solution in liters
  3. The calculator computes the molar concentration
Molarity (M) = moles of solute ÷ volume of solution (L)

Calculate Molarity from Moles and Volume

Examples

  • Example 1: Calculate molarity of 1 mole NaCl in 1 L solution
    molarity = 1 ÷ 1 = 1 M
  • Example 2: Calculate molarity of 0.5 moles HCl in 2 L solution
    molarity = 0.5 ÷ 2 = 0.25 M
  • Example 3: Calculate molarity of 2 moles NaOH in 0.5 L solution
    molarity = 2 ÷ 0.5 = 4 M

Important Notes

  • Molarity is temperature-dependent
  • Volume must be in liters
  • Molarity (M) = mol/L
  • This gives the concentration of the solution
